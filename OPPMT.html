An L5 RLSL Somatic dysfunction would most likely be associated with which of the following sacral somatic dysfunctions?





Answer:

E. Right on left sacral torsion. L5 rotates in the opposite direction as the sacrum, so L5 rotated to the left indicates the sacrum is rotated to the right.
