The major difference between psychoanalytical theories of human development and behavioral theories is that:
   
   
   
   
Answer:

D - Psychoanalytic and behavioral theories differ over the way in which they are processed. Psychoanalyticists rely on personal story and introspection, while behavioralists rely on direct observation.

Which photoreceptor cells are responsible for color vision and help create very sharp images?
   
   
   
   
Answer:

Cones are the photoreceptor cells responsible for color vision and sharp images. Rods detect lightning, while rhodopsin is a photosensitive molecule within rods that respond to light.
